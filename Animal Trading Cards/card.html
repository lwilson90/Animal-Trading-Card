Pomsky

Pomsky puppies are still quite rare to find.

  • Pedigree: Pomeranian and Siberian Husky
  • Weight: 20-30 pounds
  • Life Expectancy: 13-15 years
  • Temperament: Playful and loving

The Pomsky is a considered a new breed. Puppies are very hard to find and costly. Usually, a Husky mother and Pomeranian father are used to avoid health complications related to a small mother giving birth to large puppies.
